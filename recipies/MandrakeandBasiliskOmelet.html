Basilisk Omelet with Mandrake and Bacon

Serves: 1
Flavour: Fluffy, rich, and slightly eerie

Ingredients

  • 1 medium Mandrake (preferably with head still attached)
  • 50 grams Basilisk bacon (use the fattiest cuts)
  • 1 Basilisk egg
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Ketchup, to taste (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Mandrake: Peel and finely dice. Less bitter if harvested whole with head intact.
  2. Cook the Bacon and Mandrake: Saut√© bacon until browned. Add mandrake and cook until soft. Remove from heat.
  3. Make the Omelet Base: Beat the basilisk egg with salt and pepper until frothy.
  4. Cook the Omelet: Pour egg into hot pan. Let it set gently, keeping the top slightly runny.
  5. Fill and Fold: Add filling, fold omelet over, and finish cooking.
  6. Serve: Plate and garnish with ketchup if desired. Serve hot.
