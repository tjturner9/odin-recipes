Spit-Roasted Herb-Stuffed Basilisk

Serves: 6
Flavour: Hearty, healing, and best enjoyed after battle

Ingredients

  • 1 Basilisk (tail and innards removed)
  • Mana herb (to taste)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Stuffing

  • 1 medium Antidotal herb
  • 1 bunch Strong medicinal herb
  • 1 bunch Ultra medicinal herb
  • 1 medium Anti-paralysis herb
  • 1 medium Anti-petrify herb

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Basilisk: Clean, remove tail and innards, pat dry. Score skin and rub with salt, pepper, and mana herb.
  2. Optional – Let It Rest: Wrap and rest overnight for deeper flavour.
  3. Make the Stuffing: Chop all stuffing herbs and combine well.
  4. Stuff the Basilisk: Fill the cavity with herb mixture, evenly packed.
  5. Spit and Roast: Skewer and roast over open fire, turning regularly. Baste occasionally.
  6. Cook Low and Slow: Roast 4–6 hours until skin is crisp and meat tender.
  7. Rest and Serve: Let rest 15–30 minutes. Serve hot with wild greens or roasted roots.
